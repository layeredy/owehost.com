Our journey

We included both the positives and the negatives.


Founded

We started on January 16th, 2021 by selling game servers to the owners' friends at the lowest price possible.

Public selling

In Q2 2021 we started selling servers to people publicly at the same great low prices

Drive failure

We had our first issue running Owehost, our drive failed which caused us to lose a bit of data. We started using backups after this.

Web hosting

We expanded to web hosting in Q1 of 2022

Double digits

We had ordered our 10th server in Q4 2022

VPS hosting

We started offering VPS servers in Q1 2023.

Rebranded

We rebranded to Owehost

A slow decline

Since 2023, we have slowly been declining in users and motivation

Nearly the end

We published that Owehost will be closing in January. This was published on October 16th, 2024.

The end

We closed Owehost on January 16th, 2025. Marking our 4 year anniversary.

But maybe it's not the end...

While Owehost has closed and is no longer providing services, Layeredy (our parent company) is still working on great software and projects to help communities like yours succeed.
